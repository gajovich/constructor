A Science, Education, and Technology Ecosystem

Constructor Group, formerly known as SIT, is a global institution founded in 2019 by Dr. Serg Bell, a long-time Tech & Education entrepreneur and investor.

The main goal of the Constructor ecosystem is to address the most pressing world challenges of today:

  • Poverty & social Injustice
  • Space & universe challenges
  • Environment & sustainability
  • Wars & violence
  • Aging & diseases

Through its non-profit and for-profit entities that combine a University, a research center, businesses, consulting services, incubation, and funding, Constructor Group aims to provide innovative education that covers the entire learning lifecycle and technology breakthroughs in five fundamental technology trends:

  • Machine intelligence
  • Quantum technology
  • Intelligent materials
  • Hybrid reality
  • Life engineering

Constructor ecosystem

Constructor University

A multi-site faculty that combines the former Jacobs University Bremen (Germany), SIT campus in Schaffhausen (Switzerland), and academic partnerships with Carnegie Mellon, UniGe and NUS.

Constructor University aims to transcend academic standards through next-generation digital tools from Constructor ecosystem, interdisciplinarity, project-based learning, an entrepreneurial culture, and a research hub. The multi-disciplinary programs equip young professional elites with the right skills to meet today’s global challenges.

Constructor Learning

A one-stop destination for all management and digital technologies education. Constructor Learning’s tailor-made educational programs offer the full-cycle learning journey, from management and technology programs to consulting capabilities.

Learners are equipped with the most sought-after knowledge and skills in the market to play a leading role in their organization’s digital transformation.
